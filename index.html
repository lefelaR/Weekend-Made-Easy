🍹 English Garden

English Garden

EASY3 MIN
🥃 HIGH BALL | CUCUMBER + MINT
  • 50 ml London Dry Gin
  • 20 ml lime juice
  • 15 ml elderflower cordial
  • 3 slices cucumber
  • Top soda water

📋 STEPS: Muddle cucumber in shaker. Add gin, lime, elderflower & ice. Shake 12 sec. Double strain into highball with ice. Top soda. Garnish with cucumber ribbon & mint.

🍽️ PAIRING: Goat cheese tart · smoked salmon
🍇 Blackberry & Thyme Smash

Blackberry & Thyme Smash

EASY4 MIN
🥃 ROCKS | THYME + BLACKBERRIES
  • 50 ml Gin
  • 25 ml lemon juice
  • 20 ml honey syrup
  • 5 blackberries
  • 3 sprigs thyme

📋 STEPS: Muddle blackberries & thyme. Add gin, lemon, honey syrup & ice. Shake hard. Double strain into rocks glass over large cube. Garnish with thyme & berries.

🍽️ PAIRING: Brie cheese · dark chocolate truffles
